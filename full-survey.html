Privacy Risk Perceptions – Information for Participants

This survey is part of a study being conducted at X. We are interested in exploring how risk perceptions and privacy perceptions interact.

 

In this survey, we will ask questions based on how you use and perceive the internet, and related privacy and security management activities. You will first be asked questions that will help us understand your privacy concerns online. We will then ask questions about your general risk perceptions and behaviours.

 

Your participation in this study is completely voluntary; choosing not to take part will not disadvantage you in any way. If you do decide to take part you are still free to withdraw at any time and without giving a reason. You will receive full payment only upon completing the study.

 

The survey is anonymous and we will not collect any personal data from you.

 

The survey is expected to take approximately 17 minutes to complete.

 

The anonymous survey responses may be shared with other researchers and appear in academic publications.

 

You may contact us if you have additional questions through prolific or at xxx. If you want to raise a complaint, please contact yyy. If you feel your complaint has not been handled to your satisfaction, you can contact the Chair of the Research Ethics Committee under zzz.

 

This research project has been approved by the designated ethics officer at X.
